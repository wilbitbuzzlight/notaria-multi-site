What is Electronic Notarization?

Electronic notarization — also known as Remote Online Notarization (RON) — allows you to have your documents notarized via a secure video call without needing to come in person. You can be anywhere in the world with an internet connection and get your documents legally notarized in minutes.

This is the future of notary services, and we're bringing it to Woodside. We are currently in the approval process with our RON platform partner, BlueNotary. We'll be live soon.
