Corporate Documents

Keep your business moving, compliantly.

What Are Corporate Document Services?

Running a business means paperwork — and a lot of it needs to be legally certified. Our corporate document notarization service handles the official authentication of business formation, governance, and compliance documents, ensuring your company operates with properly verified paperwork.

Whether you're forming an LLC, executing a corporate resolution, or authenticating partnership agreements, our certified notaries are here to get it done quickly and accurately.

Documents We Notarize

  • Corporate minutes and meeting records
  • Operating agreements (LLCs)
  • Articles of Incorporation and incorporation documents
  • Business certificates and DBA filings
  • Partnership agreements
  • Corporate resolutions
  • Shareholder agreements
  • Any other official business documentation requiring notarization

Why Notarize Corporate Documents?

Notarized corporate documents provide legal proof of authenticity and protect your business in the event of disputes. Banks, government agencies, investors, and courts often require notarized documents as a condition of doing business. Having them properly certified from the start saves you complications down the road.

Fast, Walk-In Service

Bring your completed document and a valid photo ID. The authorized signatory must be present. We'll verify identity, witness the signature, and affix the notary seal — efficiently and professionally.

What to Bring

  • Completed corporate document (do not sign before arriving)
  • Valid government-issued photo ID for the authorized signatory
  • Any supporting entity documents if required
